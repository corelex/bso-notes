2. BSO Ontology - Modifications

Other potential modifications to the ontology structure:

  1. Allow disjunctions in values
  2. Allow negation
  3. Create a distinction between mandatory and optional roles
  4. Introduce the notion of lexical sets

The long-time goal is to completely merge BSO and CPA. In that case, the representation language of BSO will need to be expressive enough for all that's in CPA (or at least all the things that are worthy).

New feature:

We will introduce disjunctions for certain fields. Vetted and agreed on so far are:
  1. disjunctions over an entire subcategorisation frame
  2. disjunctions over values of individual roles

It is undecided what alternations will be represented this way, some alternations may be better expressed by another mechanism like lexical rules.

For the record: the massive amounts of exploitations in the text will not be dealt with (in disjunctions or any other vehicle), but regular alternations are fair game.

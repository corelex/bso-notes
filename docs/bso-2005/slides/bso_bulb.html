1. BSO and BULB

BSO and BULB are different things:

  1. BSO is a resource. It consists of a type system and a lexicon. There is GUI that allows us to browse BSO: the BSO Browser. The BSO Browser will be extended to include editing capabilities.
  2. BULB is a graphical user interface. It pulls together information about entries from a series of sources, including BSO, WordNet, PropBank, CPQ and others. Each of those sources has its own tab (or series of tabs) in BULB. BSO is one of the sources, and the BSO widget can choose to replicate the BSO browser. BULB is a separate effort from BSO.

Brandeis Semantic Ontology

BSO is an ontology that reflects the normal use of language as used in real text.

Development of BSO is not constrained by a customer, an intended application or anything else.

The structure of BSO is motivated by common uses of language, seen through the lense of Corpus Pattern Analysis. The BSO ontology design borrows from Generative Lexicon theory.
